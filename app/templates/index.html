Welcome to MoodTracker

Track your emotions, activities, and discover what influences your mood.

What is MoodTracker?

MoodTracker is an application designed to record and analyze your emotional state at different times of the day. Our platform allows you to:

  • Track your mood: Record how you feel at any time of the day.
  • Log activities: Keep track of the activities you perform and how they affect your mood.
  • Detailed descriptions: Add a brief description to explain what factors or events influenced your emotions.

How it works

  • Track your mood: Select from a variety of emotions to describe how you feel.
  • Add activities: Choose from a list of activities or add your own to log what you've been doing.
  • Describe your emotions: Write a brief description of what caused your mood, whether it's a specific event, interaction, or any other factor.

Benefits of using MoodTracker

  • Self-awareness: Discover patterns in your mood and better understand your emotions.
  • Well-being: Identify activities and situations that improve or negatively affect your well-being.
  • Tracking: Keep a detailed record of your emotions over time to see your emotional progress.
